The Digital Tree Physical Seed Collection

The Digital Tree Physical Seed project is a low poly abstract NFT collection with a limited supply of 722. The ultimate goal of this collection is planting 10,000 trees. Each NFT minted will result in roughly 14 trees being planted, and each tree will be tracked with MRV technology from OFP. The trees planted will be measured and their carbon offset tokenized in the form of OFP’s “Open Carbon Credit Token” which our NFT holders will receive as an airdrop. DTPS will show the true power of NFTs and NEAR.

643 digital seeds remain. Thats about 9002 physical trees left to be planted!
